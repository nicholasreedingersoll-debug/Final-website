Nirvana

Overview Pre Nevermind(1989-1990) The end of Nirvana

After their hit album "Nevermind" they hit number one on Bill Board Album Chart. Then they went to MTV where they did live shows. They then released two albums after Incesticide (1992) and In Utero (1993). Nirvana did face problems mostly Kurt Cobain and his drug use. When they performed live at the Reading August 30th 1992 Kurt Cobain decided to come out in a wheel chair to joke that the medias speculation was right about his health. In 1993 they released "Oh The Guilt" and "Puss" which were singles, They performed for a few more years and the band sadly ended in 1994 When Kurt tradically died. After Nirvana Bassist Krist Novoeselic played accordian, and Dave Grohl made the band "Foo Fighters" in 1994.
